# Close-game MLB alerts — how to join

Takes 90 seconds. No account, no subscription, no spam.

## Step 1. Install the ntfy app

- **iPhone**: App Store → search "ntfy" by Philipp Heckel → install.
- **Android**: Play Store → search "ntfy" → install. (Or F-Droid.)

## Step 2. Subscribe to your private topic

1. Open the app.
2. Tap **+** or "Subscribe to topic".
3. Type in a topic name that only you and Mitch will know. Make it long and random, for example:
   `yourname-mlb-alerts-plus-some-random-gibberish-24fg6hj8`
4. Leave the server as `ntfy.sh`.
5. Save.

*(If you want one generated for you, tell Mitch and he'll give you one.)*

## Step 3. Text Mitch three things

1. Your topic name exactly as you typed it.
2. Which teams (if any) you want alerts for. Use abbreviations:
   - All games: just say "all".
   - Specific teams: e.g. "Yankees and Red Sox" → `NYY,BOS`.
3. How close a game needs to be before you want pinged:
   - Default: tied or 1-run games.
   - Want 2-run games too? Say so.

Mitch does the rest on his end. Next time a close game hits the end of the 7th, you get a push.

## What you'll see

A push notification like:

> **Close MLB game: LAD 5 @ SD 5**
> LAD 5 @ SD 5, End 7th
> Run differential: 0
> Tap to open Gameday.

Tapping opens the MLB Gameday page for that game in your browser.

## To stop

Open the ntfy app, unsubscribe from the topic. Or tell Mitch to remove you from his list.
